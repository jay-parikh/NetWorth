# v1.2.0 — Both exchanges + dividend income

- **Prices from both stock exchanges.** Share prices now come from NSE and BSE together (NSE wins if they differ by a paisa), so your numbers match what your broker's app shows — and a share is found even if it trades on only one exchange that day.
- **Your dividends, tracked for you.** A new **Dividends** tab builds your financial-year dividend income from official company announcements: who earned it, from which share, on which date, and roughly how much. The Dashboard shows a "Dividends this FY" total and a month-by-month chart.
- **History that stays put.** When a financial year ends, its dividend rows freeze — a growing record over the years. You can always add or correct a row by hand, and your hand-typed rows are never overwritten.
- **Adding a family member is now one question.** The updater asks, you type a name, and their personal tab is built for you.
- **A friendlier Guide.** The workbook's Guide tab was rewritten in plain language with a cleaner look.

---
**Get it:** download the zip for your computer below (Windows or Mac) and unzip it. First time? Open the Excel file and read its **Guide** tab. Updating? Keep your own Excel file; just replace the *Update Portfolio* app with the new one.
