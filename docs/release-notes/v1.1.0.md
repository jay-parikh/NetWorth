# v1.1.0 — PPF done right + your money over time

NetWorth is one Excel file for your whole family's investments — shares, mutual funds, fixed deposits, PPF and bonds. You type in what you own; double-clicking **Update Portfolio** fills in the latest prices and returns. Everything stays on your own computer — no accounts, no cloud.

This is the first full release. New since the test builds:

- **PPF interest, calculated the way the Post Office does it.** Add your deposits to the PPF_Ledger tab and your balance builds itself month by month, at each quarter's official rate. (A simple balance + rate still works if you'd rather not type old deposits.)
- **Watch your net worth grow.** A new History tab and Dashboard chart record a snapshot every time you update, so you can see the family's money over time.
- **You'll know when there's a new version.** The updater checks quietly and tells you when a newer NetWorth is out.

---
**Get it:** download the zip for your computer below (Windows or Mac) and unzip it. First time? Open the Excel file and read its **Guide** tab — it walks you through everything. Updating from an older version? Keep your own Excel file; just replace the *Update Portfolio* app with the new one.
