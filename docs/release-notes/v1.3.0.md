# v1.3.0 — Your whole balance sheet

The big one: NetWorth now covers much more than market investments.

- **New asset tabs:** **EPF** (from your passbook balance and rate), **NPS** (units × the daily official NAV), **Gold & Silver** (gold bonds, coins, bars and jewellery, valued at the daily bullion rate), and **Manual Assets** for things you value yourself — property, cash, insurance, anything else.
- **Show only what you own.** A new **Settings** tab lets you switch each asset class on or off with a simple Yes/No.
- **Stay balanced.** Give each asset class a target % in Settings and the Dashboard tells you — in plain words — when you've drifted: *"Move ₹2.1L out of Equity"*. Green when you're on target, with a chart comparing actual vs target.
- **Sovereign Gold Bonds price themselves** from the market, and their 2.5% yearly interest is counted in your returns.
- **New Dashboard chart:** your net worth split by asset class, over time.

---
**Get it:** download the zip for your computer below (Windows or Mac) and unzip it. First time? Open the Excel file and read its **Guide** tab. Updating? Keep your own Excel file; just replace the *Update Portfolio* app with the new one.
