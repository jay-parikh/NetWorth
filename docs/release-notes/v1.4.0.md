# v1.4.0 — Mergers & demergers, handled for you

- **Company restructures, sorted automatically.** When a company you hold merges into another, spins off a new company, or changes its identity, the workbook now adjusts your holdings for you: the new shares appear on their own row, and your original cost and purchase dates carry over exactly the way Indian tax rules expect. You do nothing.
- **Example rows everywhere.** Every tab now ships with sample entries so you can see exactly how to fill it in. Delete the samples you don't need — the workbook tidies itself.
- **A friendlier update window.** Colourful live progress while it works, a simple menu to show or hide asset classes, and the version always shown.
- **A refreshed README** that explains the product in five minutes.

---
**Get it:** download the zip for your computer below (Windows or Mac) and unzip it. First time? Open the Excel file and read its **Guide** tab. Updating? Keep your own Excel file; just replace the *Update Portfolio* app with the new one.
